We introduce SynLogic, a novel framework for synthesizing large-scale verifiable reasoning data to improve logical reasoning capabilities in language models. Our approach enables the generation of diverse reasoning chains with verifiable correctness, facilitating more robust learning of logical reasoning patterns.
